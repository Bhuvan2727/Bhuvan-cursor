Our story

Pan Asian dining with a Black Lotus soul

In the heart of Bangalore, BLACK LOTUS KITCHEN brings the flavours of Japan, China, Korea, Thailand and beyond to one table. We craft each dish with respect for tradition and a touch of modern flair—thoughtful, seasonal and made for sharing.
