Our story

Where East meets the garden city

In the heart of Bangalore, Komorebi brings the flavours of Japan, China, Korea, Thailand and beyond to one table. We craft each dish with respect for tradition and a touch of modern flair—thoughtful, seasonal and made for sharing.
